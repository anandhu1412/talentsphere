Job Opportunities

Software Engineer

Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field. Proficiency in Java, Python, or JavaScript.

Eligibility Criteria: Minimum 2 years of experience in software development. Strong problem-solving skills.

Interview Preparation: Review algorithms and data structures. Prepare to discuss past projects and technical challenges.

Marketing Manager

Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field. Proven experience in digital marketing, SEO, and social media campaigns.

Eligibility Criteria: Minimum 5 years of experience in marketing roles. Leadership skills and ability to manage a team.

Interview Preparation: Showcase successful marketing campaigns. Discuss strategies for increasing brand visibility and customer engagement.

Internship Opportunities

Software Development Intern

Qualifications: Pursuing a degree in Computer Science or related field. Familiarity with Java or Python.

Eligibility Criteria: Enrolled in an accredited university program. Strong academic performance and eagerness to learn.

Interview Preparation: Showcase personal or academic projects related to software development. Solve coding challenges during the interview.

Marketing Intern

Qualifications: Majoring in Marketing, Business Administration, or related field. Basic understanding of digital marketing concepts.

Eligibility Criteria: Currently enrolled in university. Interest in gaining practical experience in marketing.

Interview Preparation: Highlight coursework or projects related to marketing. Be prepared to discuss social media strategies or market analysis.
